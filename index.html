Registration for the Cambridge Brainhack 2017 is now open!

The Cambridge Brainhack 2017 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2017 that is organized by Cameron Craddock . The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: March 02-04, 2017

Costs: £10 per day or £25 for all three days, includes onsite coffee, lunch, and afternoon wine reception

Location: Cambridge, UK

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2017 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
